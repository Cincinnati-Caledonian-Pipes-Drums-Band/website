Hire Band or Solo Piper

The Cincinnati Caledonian Pipes & Drums Band is available to perform at your community or private function. We are available to add the unforgettable sound of pipes and drums to all life's events:

Celebrations

Birthdays, Weddings, Anniversaries

Ceremonies

Parades and Festivals

Hiring Options

The band offers flexible options to best accomodate your special event.

The band can provide:

  • A solo performer
  • A mini-band (several pipers/drummers)
  • Entire pipe and drum corps

The band charges a fee for its appearances. The fees vary, depending on the size of the performance ensemble, location, and duration of performance. Solo pipers and drummers are typically available at an hourly rate, with additional per-member discounts for larger ensembles. For the entire band, performance fees will depend on the above factors. Please contact us for a specific quote for your event.

If you are interested in hiring the band for your next event, please contact our manager, Robert Reid. Due to the band's busy schedule, it is advised to allow at least 2 months lead time before your next event. For additional contact information, click here.
