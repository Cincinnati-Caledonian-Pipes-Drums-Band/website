2025 Cincinnati Indoor Solo Contest and Workshop

The annual Cincinnati Indoor Solo Contest and Workshop offers an EUSPBA-sanctioned piping and drumming solo contest followed by a workshop taught by the competition judges. This year we're at a venue offering superior setup for the event and convenient location (map) just off I-71 north of Cincinnati.

Registration for the competition is now closed (workshop registrations still welcome - please contact Andy Douglas at webmaster@cincypipesanddrums.org).
