Big Win at Chicago Highland Games

In the band's first outing since the pandemic, CCPD delivered a huge win at the Chicago Highland Games finishing 1st among the 12 bands competing in grade 5!

Success at Ohio Scottish Games

Just 1 week after our 1st place finish at the Chicago Highland Games, we travelled to Cleveland, Ohio for the Ohio Scottish Games and continued this year's competition successes finishing 2nd out of 8 bands competing in grade 5!

    Cincinnati's pipe band, since 1912!

    We are a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization with a mission to preserve and promote the Scottish Arts. We perform throughout the year at a wide range of community and private events, teach free lessons in Scottish bagpiping and drumming, and sponsor educational opportunities in the Scottish Arts.
